We define a parametrized collection of constraints. Below $\relof$ is a \textbf{positive integer}. In applications this positive integer will be set by the \inst{CALL}-scenario.
\[
	\justifyCalleeRevertData {i}{\relof}
	\iff
	\left\{ \begin{array}{lcl}
		\locCalleeSelfReverts & = & \cnSelfRev  _{i + \relof + 1} \\
		\locCalleeRevertStamp & = & \cnRevStamp _{i + \relof + 1} \\
	\end{array} \right.
\]
